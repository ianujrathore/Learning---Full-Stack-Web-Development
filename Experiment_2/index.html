About Delhi

Delhi, the capital city of India, is one of the oldest and most important cities in the country. It has a rich history that includes the rule of many dynasties such as the Mughals and the British. Delhi is famous for its historical monuments, busy markets, diverse culture, and delicious street food. The city is also an important center for education, politics, and business. Delhi beautifully combines ancient traditions with modern lifestyle, making it a unique and vibrant city.

Famous Food Items

Chole Bhature

Chole Bhature is one of the most popular dishes of Delhi. It is enjoyed as a spicy and filling breakfast or lunch.

Street Fast Food

Delhi is famous for its street food like momos, rolls, and chaat. These foods are tasty, affordable, and loved by people of all ages.

Parantha

Paranthas from Paranthe Wali Gali are very famous. They are stuffed with vegetables and served with butter and curd.

Tourist Spots

Red Fort

Red Fort is a historic monument built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan. It represents the rich history and architecture of India.

India Gate

India Gate is a war memorial built in honor of Indian soldiers. It is a popular tourist spot and picnic destination.

Qutub Minar

Qutub Minar is the tallest brick minaret in the world. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Festivals & Local Attractions

Diwali

Diwali is the festival of lights celebrated with joy and decorations. Homes are lit with lamps and fireworks fill the sky.

Holi

Holi is the festival of colors symbolizing happiness and unity. People celebrate by playing with colors and sweets.

Republic Day

Republic Day is celebrated on 26th January every year. A grand parade is held at Rajpath in Delhi.
